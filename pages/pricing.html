Tattoo Pricing Guide

Quality art is an investment. Our pricing reflects the skill, experience, and time dedicated to creating your perfect tattoo.

How We Price Our Tattoos

Size

Larger tattoos require more time and materials, affecting the overall cost.

Detail Level

Intricate designs with fine details take more time and precision to execute.

Color vs. Black & Grey

Color tattoos typically cost more due to additional materials and application time.

Placement

Certain body areas are more challenging to tattoo and may require additional sessions.

Pricing F.A.Q.

Do you require a deposit?

+

Yes, we require a non-refundable deposit of ₹1,000 to secure your appointment, which goes toward the final cost of your tattoo.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, and bank transfers.

Do you offer payment plans?

+

For larger pieces requiring multiple sessions, we can arrange a payment plan where you pay for each session separately.

What if I need to reschedule?

+

Please give us at least 48 hours notice to reschedule. Last-minute cancellations may result in loss of deposit.
